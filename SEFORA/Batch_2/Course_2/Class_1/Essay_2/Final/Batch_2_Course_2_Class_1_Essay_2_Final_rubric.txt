Wikipedia Edit_Final Draft

Points: 10


Please go to our Wikipedia course site and log in using your account information. 
In your edit, quote and paraphrase ideas from at least 3 recent sources. 
Your edit should be about 600-900 words (not including the reference list) and it should not include the existing passages in your assigned Wikipedia article.

After drafting your Wikipedia assignment in your sandbox, 
copy and paste your rough draft into a word document and submit the word document/pdf file here.

More information about the assignment can be found in the Wikipedia Edit assignment sheet. 
Please also check out the sample student essay.

Tips:

	In your sandbox:

		* Add citations: Select an entire sentence and click "cite" to add a citation.

		* Add hyperlinks: Select key terms and create hyperlinks to other Wikipedia pages.

		* Add headings/subheadings: Insert a heading or subheading into your edit by clicking "paragraph."


	About sources:

		* Cite at least 3 recent sources (e.g., journal articles and/or news articles) published within the past 5 years.

		* Skim through the sources and focus on paraphrasing the following sections: abstract/introduction and discussion/implications/conclusion.

		* Use signal phrases to introduce quotes/paraphrases, use quotation marks for direct quotes, and explain the quotes/paraphrases in your own words.
